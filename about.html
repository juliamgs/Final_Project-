About Me

Passionate about creating meaningful experiences through thoughtful design and development.

My Story

I'm Julia Magallanes, currently pursuing my Masters in UX Research and Design at the University of Michigan School of Information. My journey in design began with a fascination for how technology can enhance human experiences and solve real-world problems.

With a focus on inclusive design, I believe in creating digital experiences that are accessible and meaningful for everyone. My work combines user research, strategic thinking, and technical implementation to deliver solutions that truly resonate with users.

Beyond design and development, I'm passionate about accessibility advocacy and inclusive design practices. I believe technology should empower all users, regardless of their abilities or backgrounds.
